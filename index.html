Malcolm James McCormick was born on January 19, 1992,[2] in Point Breeze, Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.

He was the son of Karen Meyers, a photographer, and Mark McCormick, an architect,[4] and had an older brother, Miller. His mother is Jewish, and his father is Christian.
